1. Introduction & Background

Brainy Styles is a local clothing boutique operating in Kotuwegoda, Matara. It caters to a diverse range of customers with premium collections for men, ladies, and children. In order to match the evolving retail ecosystem, this project aims to create a web presence that acts as a digital showroom for their collections.

The digital platform allows users to explore specific sections, view item descriptions, check pricing in Sri Lankan Rupees (LKR), and find operational details such as opening hours and contact details, ensuring smooth integration between digital browsing and physical store visits.

Store Location & Operations

Address: 13A, Old Tangalle Road, Kotuwegoda, Matara.
Hours: Mon - Sat: 9:00 AM - 7:00 PM | Sun: 9:30 AM - 6:30 PM

2. Objectives

  • Modern Interface: Build an eye-catching, contemporary retail interface to showcase apparel.
  • Categorized Portals: Implement discrete sections for Kid's, Men's, and Ladies' categories.
  • Responsive Layout: Ensure standard layouts fit elegantly across a spectrum of screen dimensions.
  • Interactive UI Elements: Build custom animations, shadow effects, and glowing hover transitions.

3. Structure & Navigation Flow

The web system employs a clean, decentralized multi-page layout. The key sections are mapped as follows:

  • Home (INDEX.HTML): Main landing page introducing the brand, hours, and store location.
  • Sections Hub (Buy.html): Overview of departments (Kids, Men, Ladies) acting as a navigation hub.
  • Kids Store (Kids.html): Catalog of kids' clothing.
  • Men's Store (Boy's.html): Catalog of men's outfits.
  • Ladies Store (ladies.html): Detailed catalog of female attire and cardigans.

4. Technology Stack

Technology Component Purpose

5. Future Enhancements

  • Shopping Cart: An interactive cart where customers can collect products during their session.

  • Online Payment Gateway: Safe checkout through Payhere, PayPal, or Stripe.
